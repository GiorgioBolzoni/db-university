1. Contare quanti iscritti ci sono stati ogni anno
SELECT YEAR(`enrolment_date`) AS `enrolment_year`, COUNT(*) AS `count_of_enrolments`
FROM `students`
WHERE `enrolment_date` IS NOT NULL
GROUP BY `enrolment_year`;

        risultato:    [
            2018 -> 912
            2019 -> 1709
            2020 -> 1645
            2021 -> 734
            ]
